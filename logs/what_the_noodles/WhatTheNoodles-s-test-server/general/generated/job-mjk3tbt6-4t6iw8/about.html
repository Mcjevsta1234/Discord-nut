Our Story.

Founded in 2021, AetherNode was born out of frustration with oversold, lagging Minecraft hosts. We believed that gamers deserved better: transparent hardware specs, actual DDoS protection, and a support team that actually knows what a stack trace is.


Today, we power over 5,000 servers globally, maintaining a 99.9% uptime record and a reputation for the fastest single-thread performance in the industry.
